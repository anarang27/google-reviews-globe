# Google Reviews Globe

During my travels I've always enjoyed documenting all the places I go whether it be monuments or food places. Sooner rather than later this hobby turned into a habit.

Now, everytime before I go I enjoy researching the best places to visit in terms of sightseeing and making sure to visit the famous food joints.

However, in this world of imperfect information, there is always something you miss, and the trips we make usually don't ever come again.

Despite being an active user on Google Maps and amassing 4.9M views on my contributions, I've always wanted a cool memento in the form of a website that is personalized to me and this is my way of expressing my travel journey. I hope you enjoy :)
